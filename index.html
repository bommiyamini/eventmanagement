Our Story

As an event organization company, we believe in exploration as a force for innovation. Our passion for creative works made us start this journey in 2018. Since then we have created successful stories for more than 1000+ events. We are everything for our customers to plan their events – venue, invitation cards, floral, photography, catering, and so on. Our marketing position focuses on individuals and corporates to plan their personal, business or social events. We aim to fit our services for any budget range.

We have partnerships with more than 50 vendors across all over the Telangana & Andhra Pradesh. Our corporate office is located at Hyderabad. We continue to deliver our values to our customers through innovation, quality, accountability and value centricity.
